Full Job Description

Role Overview

  • Work as a Boilermaker across Australia's largest mine sites in the Pilbara and Goldfields regions
  • Undertake planned maintenance, shutdown and project works on fixed plant mining equipment
  • Work on equipment including crushers, conveyors, chutes, screens, bins, feeders, reclaimers and train loadouts
  • Opportunity to expand your experience and uncover your true potential with Ohlmec

Requirements

  • Australian Recognised or New Zealand Trade Qualification
  • Working at Heights certification
  • Confined Space certification
  • Construction White Card
  • Australian work rights
  • Ability to pass a pre-employment medical and ongoing drug & alcohol screens
  • Rio, FMG, Roy Hill, BHP, Newmont inductions, or inductions for Goldfields based mine sites are advantageous

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly rate from $65.00 to $73.20 per hour, plus Superannuation and local allowances
  • Ongoing work - a secure, consistent level of current and future work with Australia's biggest mining producers
  • Flexibility in work scheduling
  • Training opportunities, including High Risk tickets
  • Career Progression - Leadership crews are frequently developed from within
  • FIFO/DIDO options from Perth and various locations across the WA Pilbara and Goldfields regions
